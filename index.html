  • Golden Ager Gloomy Prospect

    Retiring in Hong Kong is stressful. Money is tight. Buddies are gone. Body is sore. Life is painful. From time to time there are visitors opening their closeted heart. They are not the long lasting friends who can celebrate birthday with, nor share the burden. The government has set series of security schemes to save them from hunger and illness. Still they are suffering. A significant amount of them opt to escape from reality.
    The project is here to talk about the hardship of what the senior residents in Hong Kong are facing, after serving the city with their youth.

    Robot caretaker in UK

    83 year old Bill has been living on his own for 7 years since his wife died. Like Bill many elderly in the UK are facing chronic loneliness. Robots is playing a role to help care for the elderly.


    Dementia Village in Netherlands

    Hogeweyk is a nursing home disguised to look like the outside world. It helps people with mild to severe dementia suffer a little bit less in their remaining years and preserves people's sense of autonomy.
